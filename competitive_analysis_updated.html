  • Technical Requirements: On-premise network control, offline capability, CJIS compliance

  • Current Technology Environment: Fragmented legacy systems, tightly controlled access, and formal procurement-based IT environments

Tertiary ICP: Healthcare & Legal Organizations

  • Organization Size: 20-500+ personnel

  • Pain Point: HIPAA/GDPR compliance, discovery redaction, evidence handling

FAQ: Common Price Objections

Q: "Our budget is only $10K."

A: Sighthound Enterprise is just $3.5K/year for 1-5 users with unlimited processing. That leaves $6.5K for training, deployment, or integration. Manual redaction at scale costs far more in labor.

Q: "Why not just hire contractors?"

A: Manual redaction is $200K-$400K/year (50+ contractors). AI is auditable, repeatable, and defensible—contractors introduce compliance risk.

Q: "Does Sighthound work on-premise?"

A: Yes. Windows, Linux, Docker, or air-gapped networks. Many government agencies use us in completely offline environments.
